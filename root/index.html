Welcome

Welcome to the Computer Science Department at Carleton College!  Within these web pages, you will find information about our program and the people within it.  Our courses range from introductions to the field, to more advanced material such as artificial intelligence, graphics, networks, and much more.  We are located in Carleton's modern Center for Mathematics and Computing (CMC).  Stop by and visit!
